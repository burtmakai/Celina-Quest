IN AN AGE OF FORGOTTEN MAGIC
Long before memory, five kingdoms carved the world from chaos — each ruled by a power older than gods, each jealously guarding its secrets.
☁️
THE SKY KINGDOM
High above the mortal realm, dragon lords soar through eternal storms. Their hoards glitter with forbidden lore — and their riddles have no mercy for the unprepared.
🌊
THE SEA KINGDOM
Beneath the black waves, sea witches weave curses into the current. Merfolk bargain in stolen breath. The depths hide wonders — and monsters — beyond imagining.
💀
THE UNDERWORLD KINGDOM
In the realm below all realms, demons feast on the living and the dead alike. Only the truly brave — or the truly desperate — venture past its gates.
🏛️
THE ANCIENT RUINS
Between kingdoms lie the shattered remains of a civilization that once ruled them all — riddled with traps, puzzles, and treasures that have outlasted empires.

ONE HERO RISES
The fate of five worlds hangs in the balance. Prove yourself worthy — and the Celestial Peak will open its gates. Are you ready?
